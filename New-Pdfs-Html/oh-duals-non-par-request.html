Ohio Provider Contract Request Form

If you are not currently a contracted provider with Molina Healthcare of Ohio and you are interested in joining our network of quality health care providers, or if you wish to add a line of business to your existing contract, please email this completed form to OHContractRequests@MolinaHealthcare.com or fax to the attention of Provider Contracts at (866) 384-1226

Please note: In order for providers to contract with a Medicaid managed care plan, the Ohio Department of Medicaid requires all providers to be enrolled with Ohio Medicaid at both the practice/facility and individual provider levels, as applicable.

If you are an individual provider joining a contracted practice, please do not complete or submit this form. Please complete and submit a Provider Information Update Form (PIF). Click here to find the form, or go to www.MolinaHealthcare.com/OhioProviders, click on the “Forms” tab and look under the “Contracted Providers Making Changes” section.
