In accordance with New York State Public Health Law Article 29-G: Regardless of population served, all telehealth-only providers must have a written procedure detailing a contingency plan in the case of a failure of transmission or other technical difficulty that renders the service undeliverable via telehealth. Policies and procedures must be available upon audit. If the service is undelivered due to a failure of transmission or other technical difficulty, a claim should not be submitted.
